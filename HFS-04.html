HFS - 04

The HFS-04 did not completely fulfill the set goals. Although the goal of simplicity was achieved, the team decided to go for a glass fiber instead of a natural fiber body during the project. In the end the car was with 224kg heavier than the set goal of 185kg. The HFS-04 project was based on extensive planning and a high production quality which caused a slight delay in finishing the car. New for the project was the main sponsorship by Brunel.
Technical specifications:
  • Engine: CBR 600 rr
  • Vehicle weight: 224 kg
  • Shifting system: Pneumatic
  • Engine management: ECU master/EMU black
  • Dampers: Tractive active dampers
  • Events attended: 1
  • Events finished: -
  • Throttle: electrical
  • Chassis: steel tubular spaceframe
  • Track width front/rear: 1250mm/1200mm
