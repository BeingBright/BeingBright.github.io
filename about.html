ABOUT US

The HAN Formula Student team is a group of students from the HAN University of Applied Sciences of Arnhem and Nijmegen who are competing in an international design competition called Formula Student.

Each year, Formula Student teams are challenged to make a fictional manufacturing company to design and develop a small formula-style racing car targeted at the nonprofessional weekend autocross enthusiasts. Based on a predetermined set of templates and rules, they must design the fastest, most effective racing vehicle possible while minimizing costs, maximizing reliability, and utilizing the latest racing technologies.

Competitions are held annually in various places all over the world (Michigan, Nebraska, Australia, Brazil, Italy, Germany, etc.) where these design teams are able to bring their car and compete against some of the best engineering students from across the planet.

The HAN Formula Student team has successfully built four vehicles so far and is currently working hard on the new challenger: the HFS-05.
