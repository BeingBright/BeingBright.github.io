CROWDFUNDING

The HAN Formula Student team is a non-profit educational organisation. The students that are part of the team are spending a lot of their free time on designing and building the HFS-05. Many parts that are designed by our team members are produced by our partners but not everything will be sponsored. Besides the partners of the HAN Formula Student team that are supporting us, the project is dependent on your support. Many team members themselves, their families, their teachers and interested people are supporting the team already, now it is your turn to become part of the crowd!

Our goal is to achieve 3000 euros with crowdfunding, follow us on social media for updates on the progress! If you would like to become part of the crowd, we are thankful for donations transferred to the HFS bank account:
