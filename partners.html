WHY CHOOSE US?

TWhy choose us? Why sponsor us? The answer to this question is simple. Every single team member is working on this project voluntarily and is enthusiastic about his work. The students of the HAN university of applied sciences of Arnhem and Nijmegen had to apply to take part in this project. We are not only working on it at school, we also work on it in our free time. The goal is to build a completely new race car based on new principles and experience and to take part in the Formula Student Germany. The HAN formula student team is the next generation of automotive engineers. Take the chance and support us with becoming a sponsor!
